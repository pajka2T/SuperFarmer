# 👨‍🌾 SuperFarmer
A digital version of the Polish board game *Super Farmer* based on the rules of the hot seat game.

-----
The entire project is available in the `final` branch.

## What is it about?
We created a digital version of the popular Polish board game called *Super Farmer*. The main goal of the game is to acquire all five species of farm animals by breeding them. These animals are - in order of rarity:
* rabbits 🐰 - the most common one
* sheep 🐑
* pigs 🐷
* cows 🐮
* horses 🐎 - the rarest one

In the game you roll the dodecahedron dices with animals and breed the drawn animal. Before your round you can exchange animals in the market - for example trade 6 rabbits for 1 sheep.

⚠️ **But be aware!**
There are also two types of predators on the dices:
* fox 🦊 - it kills almost all your rabbits leaving only one
* wolf 🐺 - it kills all your animals except rabbits

You can protect your animals from these predators by buying guard dogs in the exchange market:
* the small one 🦮 - protects you from the foxes
* the big one 🐕‍🦺 - protects you from the wolves

When the predator attacks you unfortunately lose the relevant dog.

### And who is the winner?
🏆 The person who collects all 5 species of animals first wins and can call themselves the ***Super Farmer***!
